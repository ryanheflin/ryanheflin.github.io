Welcome to Ex Profundo

I am Ryan Heflin, a soccer data analyst dedicated to transforming raw data into actionable insights that empower clubs and teams. This portfolio showcases my expertise in data analysis, visualization, and interpretation through real-world soccer data focused on the Seattle Sounders.

My Process

I follow a rigorous analytic process that includes careful problem definition, data gathering, cleaning, and exploration, followed by modeling and visualization. Each analysis is aimed at delivering clear, actionable insights tailored to decision-makers in soccer clubs.

Using a suite of tools including R, Python, Tableau, and Power BI, I create interactive dashboards and in-depth statistical visuals that facilitate understanding and strategic decision-making.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Player Performance: Detailed insights into player output and technical qualities across games and seasons.
  • Recruitment & Scouting: Advanced scouting analytics focusing on league, position, and season filters to identify top talent.
  • Tactical Analysis: Tactical breakdowns, game models, and match strategy visualizations for team and player levels.
  • Finance & Club Operations: Analytical views into player transfers, net spend, and financial efficiencies for club decision-makers.
